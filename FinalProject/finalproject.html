About Our Horses

Arabians

Arabians are known to be one of the purest and oldest horse breeds. They originate in Arabia. This Horse breed is hearty and can handle the sand from the desert and hot summer temperatures. The temperment of the Arabian can be very energetic and high-strung. They require Experienced Riders and are known to be very agile. Arabians have good endurance and speed making them champions in a wide range of competitions.

Akhal-Teke

The National Horse of Turkmanistan is the Akhal-Teke (pronounced: ahK-hail TeK-eh). This horse Breed is as ancient as the Arabian and also just as pure, being one of the first horse breeds to ever exist. People in Turkmanistan love their horses dearly and treat them better than family. This is easy to see why when you see the shiny metallic coat of this horse. The Akhal-Teke is very rare and these horses are traditionally given to royalty or important people from the Turkman people. Buying one of these horses purebred is challenging but fortunately we are able to provide you with some of the best.

Friesian

Friesians are very large Horses known for their long wavy manes and tails. They traditionally come in all black but can rarely be seen in another color. Friesians have feathering on their lower legs just above the hooves. This breed is intelligent and gentle which makes them great at dressage and also very magestic.

Miniature Horse

As the name suggests the miniature horse is a very small version of a horse. They make great companions and are good in shows. Some people train them to be seeing eye pets for the blind and can even be trained to use a toilet which makes them very intelligent and highly trainable.
